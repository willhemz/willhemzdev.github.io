About

As a young kid in 2011, I delved into learning Microsoft Office, Corel Draw and Photoshop at Eme9ky Computers Ltd, while still undertaking my secondary education. After a rigorous 6months learning program, I began my professional journey as the company's typist (I could type 100 WPM).

After completion of my secondary education, I took an upturn in my professional experience to work as a Graphics Designer and Head of Training at Delta Computers and Stores (DCS) Technologies Ltd. I co-planned with the CEO to launch the maiden edition of the Summmer Holiday One Month Free Computer Class aimed at helping students learn the use of computer accessories and to be confident in Computer Based Tests (CBT).

A few years later, I took a step-back from career world to begin my tertiary education in Mechanical Engineering at Obafemi Awolowo University. I then interned at Jubaili Bros Engineering Ltd. as a Maintenance Engineer.

Fast-forward to 2021, I transitioned into learning Web Development. I love the profession and I dedicated a large chunk of my useful hours to learning the profession. Over time, I've improved my skills and abilities as a Frontend Engineer. As a dedicated and result-oriented individual, I take pride in delivering Fast, efficient, accessible and creative solutions/experiences to one and all.

Aside my regular routine as a developer, I enjoy listening to music, playing football, making sounds on FL Studio, writing songs and doing computational research on flow field.
